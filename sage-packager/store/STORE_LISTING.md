## Full description
SAGE (smart interactive reminder) turns a quick note into a smart, shareable reminder.

• Speak or type — SAGE understands dates, times, places, and people from natural language.
• Add a location and SAGE maps it, with live GPS routing for whoever needs to get there.
• Send an interactive reminder link by text or email — recipients can open it, adjust the schedule or location, and track the route on their own device.
• Clean, mobile-first design with a compact voice mode and a full standard mode for manual entry.
• Optional supporter contribution to help keep SAGE growing.

No clutter, no accounts required to receive a reminder — just tap the link and go.
